Learn how to use Sign Language Translator

Step 1:

To start off, make sure the your device has allowed camera access to this web browser (you can change this in Setting).

Step 2:

Once access is allowed, select your desired translated language. CC is automatic.

Step 3:

Select verbal translation if desired. Now you can prop your device at an angle that will capture hand movement.

Step 4:

Begin live-recording and enjoy real-time translation!
