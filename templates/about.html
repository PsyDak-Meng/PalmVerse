Our goal is to help families, friends, and colleugues communicate more effectively. Million of Americans, and billions world-wide have a language barrier due to communication disabilities. Our program facilitiates this by interpreting ASL hand movements and converting it into text-captions. We can even go as far as converting it into audio! We use a combination of alphabetical and word signs to make our communication more vast.
